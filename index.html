Phrases That Matter
Printable solo travel language worksheet
Minimal prep. Maximum smoothness.

Learn the few phrases that actually help.

This is a simple, printable worksheet to jot down the local-language phrases you’ll use most. Not fluent. Not perfect. Just prepared.

Download the PDF
Tip: Save it offline on your phone. Screenshot works too.
Print suggestion: 100% scale (don’t “fit to page”).
How to use it
  • Pick the phrases that matter most for your trip and fill in the local-language column.
  • Keep it somewhere easy to access: phone photos, Notes app, or folded in your day bag.
  • Don’t stress pronunciation. Effort and politeness beat perfection.
  • Focus on phrases that unlock real-world interactions: greetings, help, food, directions, money.
