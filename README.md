# Phrases That Matter – Printable Solo Travel Worksheet

A clean, printable language worksheet designed for solo travelers.

This resource accompanies *You’re Going Alone?!* and helps travelers prepare a small set of useful phrases in the local language before their trip.

Minimal prep. Maximum smoothness.

---

## What This Is

A simple, printable PDF that includes space to write local-language translations for:

- Politeness & basic greetings  
- Asking for help or clarification  
- Food & ordering  
- Directions & getting around  
- Money & shopping  
- Logistics & safety  

The goal isn’t fluency.  
It’s confidence and smoother real-world interactions.

---

## How to Use

1. Download the PDF.
2. Fill in the “Local Language” column before your trip.
3. Print it, screenshot it, or save it offline.
4. Focus on effort and politeness over perfect pronunciation.

A few well-used phrases go further than a memorized phrasebook.
